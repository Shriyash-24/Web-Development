How To Prepare For GATE?

  1. Why To Prepare?
  2. Why Not To Prepare

Why To Prepare?

  • If you belong to tier 3 colleges which doesn't provide placements.
  • You want to persue higher studies in IITs, IIsC.
  • If you are preparing for PSUs.

Why Not to Prepare?

  • If you are not interested in working in core fields.
  • If you want to prepare for a teacher at coaching institutes.
  • If you are just looking for enjoyment.
