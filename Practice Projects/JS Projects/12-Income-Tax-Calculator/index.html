The calculations are calculated based on the new tax regime in India.The tax is deducted based on the slab under the new tax regime, i.e., up to Rs. 3,00,000 is nil, from Rs. 3,00,000 to Rs. 7,00,000 is 5%, from Rs. 7,00,001 to Rs. 10,00,000 is 10%, from Rs. 10,00,001 to Rs. 12,00,000 is 15%, from Rs. 12,00,001 to Rs. 15,00,000 is 20%, and above Rs. 15,00,000 is 30%.
